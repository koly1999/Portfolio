Electric Car

An autonomous electric car prototype that stops itself at a preset distance—traction, gearing, and braking engineered from the ground up.

Early Prototype – Traction Issues

First prototype suffered from poor traction and wandering off-course.

Gearing & Timing

Added a geared timing counter for distance measurement, bit it proved unreliable in field tests.

Foam Wheels & Threaded Counter

Switched to foam wheels for grip, introduced a threaded distance encoder for more consistent readings.

Final Version – Chassis & Brakes

Upgraded electronics, disk brakes, and custom 3D-printed chassis—now drives straight like a drag racer.
