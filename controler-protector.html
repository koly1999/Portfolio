Gimbal Protector

Custom 3D-printed joystick guards for RC controllers—designed to prevent damage and ensure drop-tested durability.

Initial CAD Model

First computer design featured an “H” motif inspired by the HobbyTown logo—laid the groundwork for function tests.

Refined Prototype Drawing

Printed prototypes in ABS and performed drop tests from 10 ft to validate impact resistance.

In-Store Deployment

Mounted protectors on demonstration units at HobbyTown USA to showcase real-world durability and fit.

Final Production

Delivered multi-color batches per request—finalized design, documentation, and manufacturing process, it was a lot of fun!
