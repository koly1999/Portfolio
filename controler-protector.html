Remote Control Gimbal Protector

I used to work at a hobby store called Hobbytown USA, and my boss at the time asked me to design and 3D print him these things called gimball protectors. Gimball protectors make it really difficult to damage the joysticks of a remote controller. These controllers were relativley expensive so protecting them was important.

This is my first CAD model of the Protectors, the H was in the style of the stores logo.

This is my second CAD drawing of the protectors, they required some changing of dimensions after the first ptototype failed.

This is the first batch of sucessful gimball protectors.

Here are my gimball protectors in action at the store.

Once I finalised the design with my former boss, I then sold him a few batches in various colours.
