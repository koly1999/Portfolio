Phone Holder

I needed a Phone holder for my Mini Cooper, so I designed and 3D printed a custom mount that fits perfectly into the air-vent. The design features a ball-joint for easy adjustment and a cradle that securely holds the phone in place. It took an existing phone holder design and improved it.
