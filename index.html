About Me

“A jack of all trades is a master of none… often better than a master of one.”

Built to Make

My dad trained at London’s Royal College of Art—and made sure I learned every trade: wood, metal, electronics and more. From day one, I’ve lived in the workshop.

Mini Cooper Mechanic

My ’06 Mini was always breaking—so I fixed brakes, wiring, sensors, suspension, engine components, and more, until maintenance costs made me sell it.

From Minecraft to Servers

I started by hosting Minecraft realms for friends—then built custom gaming PCs, NAS boxes and Linux clusters to keep them online.

Science Olympiad Winner

In high school, I led my team in seven build events—gliders, hovercraft, electric cars, wind turbines—and won regionals, plus three medals at Illinois invitational.

Curriculum & Beyond

I earned an A.A. in engineering & electronics, and now I’m completing my B.S. in full-stack web dev & graphic design at ASU Online.
